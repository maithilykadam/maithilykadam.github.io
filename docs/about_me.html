Hi there!

My name is Maithily Kadam, and I am a student in the Management Engineering program at the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about UI/UX design and enjoy creating intuitive and engaging user experiences for various digital products.

My interest in UI/UX design started during my high school years when I was involved in creating logos and digital posters for school projects. I was then taken to a UX Design workshop and was fascinated by the impact that design can have on a user's interaction with technology. It can make the difference between frustration and ease of use, and ultimately, influence how people perceive a product.

I'm improving my UI/UX design skills through university design challenges and exploring new software. I'm also excited about my upcoming Human-Computer Interaction course, which will deepen my understanding of how technology impacts UI/UX design and user experience.

Staying active and playing sports, particularly volleyball, is a favorite pastime of mine. It serves as an ideal outlet for me to unwind after a busy day, while also allowing me to connect with my closest friends in a fun and competitive environment.
